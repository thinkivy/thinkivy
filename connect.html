How Does This Work?

Initial Assessment Report

Candidates interested in the program will fill out the report so that they can be matched with a potential mentor.

Free 30-minute Demo Session

Matched mentor will speak with potential student over video call to demonstrate services.

Initial Assessment Report

Receive a FREE Initial Assessment Report based on student scores and hard skills.

Comprehensive Assessment Report

Receive a qualitative assessment based on student personality and soft skills.

Five Online Sessions

Work 1-on-1 with mentors over five sessions interspersed with feedback reports & homework.

Final Assessment Report

Receive end of service report for student with suggestions for success.

Students Will Walk Away With:

  • Initial, Comprehensive & Final Assessment Reports
  • Insights and value of working with a mentor for 5+ hours online.
  • Mentor revised work, such as essays, personal statements, interview responses, etc.
  • A renewed confidence and understanding of the college application process.
